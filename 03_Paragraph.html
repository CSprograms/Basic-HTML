Paragraph 1 :

The HTML Paragraph element represents a paragraph. Paragraphs are usually represented in visual media as blocks of text separated from adjacent blocks by blank lines and/or first-line indentation. But HTML paragraphs can be any structural grouping of related content, such as images or form fields.
